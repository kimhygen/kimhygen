Welcome

My name is Kim Langevin. I reside in Sault Ste Marie, Ontario where I work as an independent dental hygienist from my residential office to provide registered dental hygiene services to those who need it. I can take same week appointments and my hours are accommodating in order to fit your exact needs: So text or call to book your appointment today!

I also work from the Thessalon Dental Center and several local First Nations locations. I strive to accommodate all of my patients' needs and I make sure that I offer a broad range of locations so that oral health care can be accessible to everyone.
